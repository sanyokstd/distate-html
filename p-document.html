DiState: Document flow

Local solution for converting paper workflow into electronic form without losses legal significance. Companies of any size and organizational structure can automate:

Internal document flow

with employees and branches

External document flow

with clients, contractors and partners

Possibilities
Free document management
Own EDI service saves you and your counterparties from costs for billing document flow
Exchange of any documents
Upload any documents to the system or create UPD, UCD, Torg-12, acts and invoice templates
Single local archive
The solution is installed on your servers, where all electronic documents companies
Legal significance of all documents
Any qualified electronic signature, including for sending reporting, suitable for the system. Documents signed by the CEP are accepted by the courts and the Federal Tax Service in as originals
Extensive scaling options
Automate not only document flow, but also your business processes: agreement, reconciliation, automatic sending and signing, sorting, routing, etc.
Confidentiality of all transmitted data
Local installation of the solution protects documents and data from access third parties and EDI operator, and setting rights and roles allows employees to work only with documents available to them
For whom
Small business Retail Banking sector rental business Telecom Production Auto industry Logistics
Functionality
Documentation Cabinet Company Counterparties
Documentation
  • Uploading any documents and creating from templates
  • Co-authoring drafts
  • Signing of CEP documents
  • Tracking the status in the system or by protocol
  • Creating personal document folders
  • Search for documents by any parameters
  • Downloading and printing documents from the EDI system
Cabinet
  • Authorization by CEP or login and password
  • Setting alerts for incoming documents
  • Use of multiple CEP certificates
Company
  • Activating or deleting employee accounts
  • Flexible configuration of access rights and roles for working in the system
Counterparties
  • Creating groups of contractors and assigning employees to work with them
  • Sending invitations, activating or blocking counterparties
