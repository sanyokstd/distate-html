DiState Co.

The company "DiState" was founded in 2008 and is one of the first developers of software provision in areas of electronic legally significant document management

We create software solutions that automate business processes equally effectively as in major companies, as well as small and medium-sized businesses. Our products help companies create them own services of legally significant document flow, integrate EDI with accounting systems, exchange documents with any EDI systems without roaming and solve other tasks without development costs new solutions.

During our existence, we have accumulated significant experience in the development and implementation of systems electronic document management in various industries. This experience allows us to improve existing program products and create new relevant solutions to optimize the activities of enterprises various sizes and types activities.
