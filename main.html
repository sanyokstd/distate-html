Ready-made solutions for automation of electronic document management

Organize convenient work with electronic documents and set up EDI for business processes DiState

Electronic document management

A fast and inexpensive way to exchange documents with contractors, clients and employees. EDI accelerates business processes of the company: conclusion of transactions, settlements, registration of personnel procedures and others. Electronic documents are not lost during delivery and are easy to find in the archive.

Qualified Electronic Signature (QES) provides legal validity and protection document from forgery. Accounting, personnel and internal electronic documents do not require duplication on paper - they can provide during inspections and refer to the courts.

Our advantages
local archive
All documents are stored on the company's server in a single local archive with functions search, filters, administration of access rights and employee roles.
Safety
The company sets the level of system protection: configures the backup copy, access servers and databases in accordance with the information security regulations.
Free document management
Documents are not charged in DiState solutions. create, sign and exchange electronic documents with counterparties and within the company for free.
Scalability
The unique architecture of DiState products gives wide scaling options, integration and automation of the company's business processes.
privacy
Exchange electronic documents in your own service edo and save privacy of commercial information from third parties, including from the EDF operator.
Collaboration on documents
Unsent documents are stored in the Drafts section. Company employees can change, view, sign and send them to the counterparty.
