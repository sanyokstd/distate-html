A local solution for working with data from different EDI systems, EDI systems and ERP of the company in the "one window". Behind the integration of data streams corresponds to the integration bus. Automatic processing services, routing, reconciliation and signing of electronic documents remove the load from corporate systems.

Connectors

Connectors provide data exchange between personal accounts of the company in other EDI and EDI systems and DiState: Multiprovider. They are responsible for receiving and sending documents.

Coordination

The company determines the document approval route. Before the final signing and sending documents are subject to internal approval.

Routing

The document is checked for errors and sent further according to the specified route company.

Autosign

Allows you to automatically sign and send back incoming documentation.

Multisignature

Allows you to use several CEPs for signing documents and deactivate them if necessary.

Reconciliation of documents

The incoming document is checked for compliance with the specified parameters, and then sent to the counterparty or to manual check.

Own EDI service

An alternative solution to exchange through connectors is its own EDI service. Contractors not connected to operators will be able to exchange documents with you for free on your site.

Advantages
EDI optimization
  • Multiple EDI and EDI streams in one solution
  • Connector to any EDI/EDI system
  • Reduced load on company systems

  • Open APIs and modules for integration
  • Receiving, checking, sending and archiving documents
