DiState: Personnel

Turnkey solution for registration of personnel procedures in electronic form with application qualified electronic signature (QES).

CEP ensures the legal significance of all HR documents signed in the system, in including contracts.

Working with the system is possible from anywhere in the world, therefore, for registration of the personnel procedure not necessary visit the office.

The system automatically generates SZV-TD reports for each HR activity and prepares them to be sent to Pension Fund.
